Frame 02 About the Club

Named after the magic lantern

A film club built around discovery, atmosphere, and shared taste.

The name comes from one of cinema's earliest projection devices. The club keeps that sense of shared wonder alive with programming that moves between landmarks, discoveries, and contemporary film culture.

NO

Vi viser filmperler som underholder, engasjerer og inspirerer, og bygger et sosialt møtested for filminteresserte i Stavanger.

EN

Expect a welcoming room for people who actually want to watch carefully and talk afterwards.

Programming

Classics, discoveries, newer film art.

The programme is curated for curiosity rather than for safe consensus.

Atmosphere

A club night, not a conveyor belt.

Popcorn is free, the bar is open, and the evening leaves room for conversation.

Audience

International, local, warm.

NFK has highlighted the club's unusually mixed and welcoming audience.

Frame 03 Venue

Screenings at Folken

Historic room, proper projection, low-friction club night.

  • Main hall at Folken in central Stavanger.
  • Supports digital, 35mm and 16mm projection.
  • Screen measures roughly 7 by 4 metres.
  • Free popcorn every screening, with the bar open.
  • Rights are cleared through Norsk Filmklubbforbund.

Frame 08 Community

Volunteer-run with serious taste

The club keeps its own personality while staying connected to a wider film-club movement.

Recognition

Film Club of the Year 2023.

Norsk Filmklubbforbund highlighted the programming range, atmosphere, and unusually international audience mix.

People

Volunteer energy.

The screenings feel personal because the people behind them care about curation, hospitality, and keeping film culture accessible.

Network

Connected through NFK.

Rights and logistics are supported nationally, while the club still feels local, idiosyncratic, and independent.
