Frame 02 About the Club

Named after the magic lantern

A film club built for people who want more than a standard cinema night.

The name comes from one of cinema's earliest projection devices. The club keeps that feeling of shared discovery alive with adventurous programming, a social room, and screenings chosen to reward curiosity.

NO

Vi viser filmperler som underholder, engasjerer og inspirerer, og bygger et sosialt møtested for filminteresserte i Stavanger.

EN

Expect classics, discoveries, and newer film art, plus a room where people actually stay to talk after the credits.

Programming

Curated, not generic.

Landmarks sit next to lesser-known work and contemporary films that rarely land in ordinary multiplex schedules.

Atmosphere

Social, not transactional.

Free popcorn, an open bar, and enough room around the screening to make the night feel communal.

Audience

Local and international.

NFK has singled out the club for its warm mix of regulars, newcomers, Stavanger locals, and international film lovers.

Frame 03 Venue

Screenings at Folken

Historic room, proper projection, club-night warmth.

  • Main hall at Folken in central Stavanger.
  • Supports digital, 35mm and 16mm projection.
  • Large screen measuring roughly 7 by 4 metres.
  • Free popcorn every screening, with the bar open all evening.
  • Rights are cleared through Norsk Filmklubbforbund.
