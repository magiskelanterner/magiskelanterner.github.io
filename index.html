About the club

Named after the magic lantern.

The club takes its name from the early projection device that made moving images feel uncanny, shared and alive.1

People

A volunteer-run club with serious taste.

Our warm atmosphere comes from volunteers who care about curation, hospitality and accessibility.

Community

International, local, curious.

Our movie evenings are a meeting point for film lovers, newcomers and regulars alike.

Network

A truly international film culture.

Belonging to the Norsk Filmklubbforbund network ensures our screenings run with proper rights clearance.

Programming

Classics, discoveries, newer film art.

The club mixes beloved landmarks with lesser-known works and contemporary films beyond a standard cinema schedule.
