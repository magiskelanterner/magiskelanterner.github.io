About the club

Named after the magic lantern.

The club takes its name from the early projection device that made moving images feel uncanny, shared and alive.

Programming

Classics, discoveries, newer film art.

The club mixes beloved landmarks with lesser-known works and contemporary films beyond a standard cinema schedule.

Atmosphere

A social screening night.

Free popcorn, open bar, and evenings designed to feel like a meeting point for film lovers.

Audience

International, local, curious.

NFK has highlighted the club's warm, international community. A place for newcomers and regulars alike.

Screenings

At Folken's historic main hall.

Big-screen cinema with club-night warmth.

  • Main hall at Folken in central Stavanger.
  • Professional projection for digital, 35mm and 16mm.
  • A screen roughly 7 by 4 metres.
  • Free popcorn at every screening.
  • Open bar, BYO snacks welcome.

Formats

Digital + analogue

35mm and 16mm projection alongside digital.

Experience

Popcorn included

Welcoming film nights with space to linger.

Folken, Stavanger
