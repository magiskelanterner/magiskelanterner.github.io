About the club

Named after the magic lantern.

The club takes its name from the early projection device that made moving images feel uncanny, shared and alive. That same atmosphere shapes the screenings today: adventurous programming, a social room, and films worth discovering together.

NO

Vi viser filmperler som underholder, engasjerer og inspirerer, og bygger et sosialt møtested for filminteresserte i Stavanger.

EN

Expect carefully curated films that reward curiosity, plus a welcoming room for people who actually want to talk about what they watched.

Programming

Classics, discoveries, newer film art.

The club mixes beloved landmarks with lesser-known works and contemporary films that usually fall outside a standard cinema schedule.

Atmosphere

A social screening night, not just a screening.

Popcorn is free, the bar is open, and the evening is designed to feel like a meeting point for film lovers rather than a fast in-and-out show.

Audience

International, local, curious.

NFK has highlighted the club's warm and international community. It is a place for newcomers, regulars and everyone in between.

Screenings

At Folken's historic main hall.

Screenings take place at Folken, in a room connected to Stavanger's early cinema history and equipped for both digital and analogue projection.

The room

Big-screen cinema with club-night warmth.

  • Main hall at Folken in central Stavanger.
  • Professional projection for digital, 35mm and 16mm formats.
  • A screen measuring roughly 7 by 4 metres.
  • Free popcorn at every screening.
  • The bar is open, and guests can bring their own snacks.
  • Screening rights are cleared through Norsk Filmklubbforbund.

Membership and tickets

Simple, affordable, built for repeat visits.

Membership is part of the film-club model. Join once per season, then pay a low ticket price whenever you come to a screening.

Membership

Mandatory season pass

80 NOK

Register through the official Filmklubbmedlem portal before your first screening of the season.

One membership per season.

Ticket

Per screening

40 NOK

Tickets are paid at Folken. Card and Vipps are accepted at the door.

Low commitment, easy to drop in.

How it works

Three quick steps

    Register as a member for the current season.

  2. 2

    Show up at Folken and pay 40 NOK for the screening.

Community
